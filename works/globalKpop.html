Description

The main objective of the project was to find a topic that had significant global impact and had datasets that could be used to showcase the data. Taking the data provided from multiple websites different graphs and charts were created to display and simplify the data. The topic of choice was Korean pop, or "K-Pop" and how the music genre has made a significant impact on the world, economically and socially.
